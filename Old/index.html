About

I'm a creative technologist with a fire for solving real problems. I use NLP, APIs, and immersive tech to build tools that improve lives—especially in education, language access, and user experience.

I care deeply about who gets left behind when technology isn’t designed with people in mind. That’s why my work blends creativity, cultural insight, and technical skill.

Highlighted Projects & Experience:

  • Game Tester – ZeniMax / I.D. Software (2019): Participated in pre-launch QA and gameplay testing for AAA titles; logged bugs and verified fixes across multiple build cycles.
  • CRM Implementation – Class AAA LEED Building (2019): Rolled out CRM for 800 commercial tenants; streamlined training and long-term support.
  • Volunteer Translator – Food Matters / North Texas Food Bank (2023): Live translation for Spanish- and English-speaking families in a nutrition equity program.
  • Chinese Localization Project (Summer 2024): Translated and culturally adapted Chinese magazine content for bilingual publication.
  • Ignite Fellow – Teach for America (Fall 2025): Selected to support underserved K–12 students through virtual, culturally responsive instruction.
  • Global Career Accelerator – UTA (Summer 2025): Built front-end apps using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and AI. Led a design sprint for charity:water; earned certifications in AI-powered JS, UX, and intercultural communication.

I design and developing interactive learning tools with NLP, 3D modeling, and intuitive front-end systems—because tech should speak your language.
